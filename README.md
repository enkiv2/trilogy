After the events of [Manna for our Malices](http://github.com/enkiv2/mannaforourmalices) and [Book of the Damned](https://github.com/enkiv2/misc/blob/master/Book%20of%20the%20Damned), Akagi Ai and her friends get stuck in the rain with Yumeji Misato and Bruno Akane, and they tell ghost stories in this short non-interactive VN.

Trilogy is [available from itch.io](https://enkiv2.itch.io/trilogy), or you can use ren'py to build this source code.
